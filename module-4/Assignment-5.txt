Networking Challenges Report

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will research and write a comprehensive report detailing common challenges faced by networking layers in blockchain systems. They should provide examples and recommend strategies to mitigate these challenges. This exercise encourages students to gain a deeper understanding of the practical issues involved in blockchain networking.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to Networking Challenges (15 minutes)

Introduce students to the importance of understanding and addressing common challenges in blockchain networking layers and their impact on the overall performance and security of blockchain systems.
Task 2: Research and Analysis (2.5 - 3.5 hours)
2. Instruct students to conduct research on common challenges faced by networking layers in blockchain systems. They should focus on the following aspects:

Challenges Identification (30%):

Identify and describe common challenges, such as latency, scalability issues, security threats, and network congestion, faced by networking layers in blockchain systems.
Examples and Case Studies (40%):

Provide real-world examples and case studies illustrating instances where these challenges have had a significant impact on blockchain networks.
Mitigation Strategies (30%):

Recommend strategies and solutions to mitigate or address these challenges effectively. Discuss technical, governance, and operational approaches.
Task 3: Report Writing (30 minutes - 1 hour)
3. Ask students to compile their research findings into a comprehensive report. Reports should include:

Introduction (10%):

Provide an introduction to the topic and the purpose of the report.
Challenges Identification (30%):

Present findings on common challenges faced by networking layers in blockchain systems.
Examples and Case Studies (40%):

Discuss real-world examples and case studies illustrating these challenges.
Mitigation Strategies (20%):

Recommend strategies and solutions to mitigate the identified challenges.
Task 4: Peer Review (1 hour)
4. Organize a peer review session where students exchange their reports for feedback. Encourage constructive criticism and suggestions for improvement.

Task 5: Revision (30 minutes - 1 hour)
5. Based on the feedback received during the peer review, ask students to revise and refine their reports.

Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their finalized reports.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Depth and accuracy of the challenges identification and analysis.
Clarity and organization of the report.
Quality of the recommended mitigation strategies.
