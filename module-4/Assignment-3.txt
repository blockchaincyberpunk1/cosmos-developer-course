Governance Process Analysis

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will analyze the governance process of a selected blockchain project. They will choose a project, study its governance history, and write an analysis of the strengths and weaknesses in managing upgrades and decisions through their governance mechanisms.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to Governance Analysis (15 minutes)

Introduce students to the concept of governance analysis in blockchain projects and its importance in understanding the effectiveness of decentralized decision-making.
Task 2: Blockchain Project Selection (15 minutes)
2. Instruct students to choose a specific blockchain project for their analysis. They should select a project that has a notable history of governance-related decisions and upgrades. Provide a list of suggested projects, or allow students to choose their own.

Task 3: Governance Analysis (1.5 - 2.5 hours)
3. Ask students to perform a comprehensive analysis of the governance process of the selected blockchain project. They should focus on the following aspects:

Governance Mechanisms (30%):

Describe the governance mechanisms used by the project, including on-chain voting, decision-making structures, and token-based governance.
Explain how stakeholders are involved in the governance process.
Upgrade and Decision History (40%):

Research and document the history of major upgrades, protocol changes, and significant decisions made through governance.
Include details on the proposals, discussions, voting outcomes, and implementation of changes.
Strengths and Weaknesses (20%):

Analyze the strengths and weaknesses of the project's governance process. What aspects of the governance process have worked well, and which ones have presented challenges?
Consider factors such as transparency, inclusiveness, efficiency, and security.
Recommendations (10%):

Based on the analysis, provide recommendations for improvements or changes in the project's governance process.
Task 4: Report Writing (1-1.5 hours)
4. Ask students to write a detailed report summarizing their governance process analysis. Reports should include:

Introduction (10%):

Provide an introduction to the selected blockchain project and the purpose of the analysis.
Governance Process Analysis (70%):

Present findings and insights from the analysis, focusing on governance mechanisms, upgrade and decision history, strengths, and weaknesses.
Recommendations (10%):

Offer recommendations for enhancing the project's governance process based on the analysis.
Conclusion (10%):

Summarize the key points of the analysis and the importance of effective governance in blockchain projects.
Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their governance process analysis reports.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Depth and accuracy of the governance analysis.
Clarity and organization of the report.
Thoughtfulness in providing recommendations.
