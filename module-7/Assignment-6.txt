Application Showcase

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will create a presentation or demo video to showcase the advanced features and cross-chain interactions of their blockchain applications. The goal is to demonstrate how their applications leverage the full capabilities of the Cosmos SDK and the broader Cosmos ecosystem.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to Application Showcase (15 minutes)

Introduce students to the significance of showcasing their advanced blockchain applications, emphasizing the importance of effectively communicating their project's capabilities.
Task 2: Showcase Preparation (2 - 3 hours)
2. Challenge students to prepare a presentation or demo video showcasing the advanced features and cross-chain interactions of their blockchain applications. The showcase should include the following elements:

Introduction (10%):

Briefly introduce the project and its objectives.
Advanced Features (30%):

Highlight the advanced modules or features implemented in the application.
Provide a detailed explanation of how these features work and their benefits.
Cross-Chain Interactions (30%):

Demonstrate how the application interacts with other chains within the Cosmos ecosystem using the IBC protocol or other relevant technologies.
Explain the use cases and advantages of cross-chain communication.
Live Demo (20%):

If possible, showcase a live demonstration of the application in action, emphasizing its functionality and usability.
Q&A and Discussion (10%):

Encourage questions and discussion from the audience or peers.
Task 3: Presentation or Demo Creation (2 - 3 hours)
3. Instruct students to create their presentation slides or record a demo video, ensuring that they cover all the elements mentioned above.

Task 4: Submission (30 minutes - 1 hour)
4. Request students to submit their presentation slides or demo videos, ensuring that all relevant content is included.

Task 5: Peer Review and Feedback (1 hour)
5. Organize a peer review session where students exchange their presentation slides or demo videos for feedback. Encourage constructive criticism and suggestions for improvement.

Task 6: Revision (30 minutes - 1 hour)
6. Based on the feedback received during the peer review, ask students to revise and refine their presentation slides or demo videos.

Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their finalized presentation slides or demo videos.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Clarity and completeness of the presentation or demo, including coverage of advanced features and cross-chain interactions.
Quality of the live demonstration (if applicable).
Engagement with the audience or peers during the Q&A and discussion.
