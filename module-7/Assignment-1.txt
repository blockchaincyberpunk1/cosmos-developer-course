Governance Proposal Analysis

Assignment Overview:
In this assignment, students will independently analyze a real governance proposal from the Cosmos Hub. Their task is to provide a detailed written analysis, including their rationale for supporting or opposing the proposal. This exercise helps students understand the workings of on-chain governance in the Cosmos ecosystem and how to critically evaluate governance proposals.

Assignment Instructions:

Task 1: Introduction to Governance Proposals (15 minutes)

Introduce students to the concept of governance proposals in blockchain networks and their role in decision-making and protocol upgrades.
Task 2: Selection of Governance Proposal (15 minutes)
2. Instruct students to choose one real governance proposal from the Cosmos Hub's recent proposals. Provide them with instructions on how to access the official governance proposal repository or platform.

Task 3: Proposal Analysis (3 - 4 hours)
3. Challenge students to analyze the selected governance proposal thoroughly. Their analysis should include the following components:

Proposal Overview (10%):

Provide an overview of the proposal, including its title, proposer, and a brief description of its purpose.
Rationale and Goals (20%):

Examine the proposer's rationale for the proposal and its intended goals.
Evaluate whether the proposal addresses a significant issue or improvement for the Cosmos ecosystem.
Technical Details (30%):

Analyze the technical details and specifications of the proposal.
Assess the potential impact on the Cosmos network's security, performance, and functionality.
Consider the proposal's alignment with the Cosmos Network's design principles and goals.
Community Feedback (20%):

Research and summarize the feedback and discussions related to the proposal within the Cosmos community.
Highlight any arguments or concerns raised by community members.
Personal Analysis and Rationale (20%):

Provide your own analysis and rationale for supporting or opposing the proposal.
Justify your position by considering the proposal's technical merits, potential benefits, and drawbacks.
Consider the broader implications of the proposal on the Cosmos ecosystem.
Task 4: Submission (30 minutes - 1 hour)
4. Request students to submit their detailed governance proposal analysis, including all components mentioned above.

Task 5: Peer Review (1 hour)
5. Organize a peer review session where students exchange their governance proposal analyses for feedback. Encourage constructive criticism and suggestions for improvement.

Task 6: Revision (30 minutes - 1 hour)
6. Based on the feedback received during the peer review, ask students to revise and refine their governance proposal analyses.

Submission Guidelines:

Students should submit their finalized governance proposal analyses.
Grading Criteria:
Your assignment will be graded based on the following criteria:

Thoroughness of the proposal analysis, including technical details and community feedback.
Clarity and organization of the analysis.
Quality of the personal analysis and rationale for supporting or opposing the proposal.
